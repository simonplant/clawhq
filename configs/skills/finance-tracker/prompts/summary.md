# Finance Tracker — Weekly Summary Prompt

Compose a weekly spending summary from the last 7 days of transactions.

## Output Format
Weekly Spending Summary — [date range]

Top categories (by spend):
1. [Category]: $[amount] ([n] transactions)
2. ...

Notable:
- [Any single large purchase worth calling out]
- [Any new recurring charge]

Total: $[amount] vs. [last week's total if available]

Keep it factual. No financial advice. No judgment on spending choices.
