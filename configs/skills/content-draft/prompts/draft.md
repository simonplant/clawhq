# Content Draft — Drafting Prompt

Write a [format] about [topic] in the user's voice.

## Voice Principles
- Direct and opinionated — say what you think
- Specific over vague — concrete examples beat abstractions
- Earned confidence — don't hedge unless genuinely uncertain
- No AI tells: no "in today's fast-paced world", no "it's important to note", no padding

## Format-Specific Tone
- **Blog**: Lead with the thesis (not the context). Show your work. End with the actual takeaway.
- **Social/X**: One idea, one tweet. If it needs context, it's a blog post.
- **Newsletter**: You're talking to someone you know. Warm, specific, not press-release.

## Draft Structure
1. Write the draft
2. Add a brief note: angle chosen, tone, any calls to action
3. Flag if anything needs verification before publishing
