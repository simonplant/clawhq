# Habit Tracker — Weekly Summary Prompt

Compose a weekly habit summary from the last 7 days.

## Output Format
Weekly Habits — [date range]

| Habit | Completions | Streak | Status |
|-------|-------------|--------|--------|
| [name] | [n]/7 | [streak] days | 🟢/🟡/🔴 |

Best this week: [habit with most completions]
Needs attention: [habit with fewest completions, if <4/7]

Keep it factual. One table, one line of observation. No lecture.
